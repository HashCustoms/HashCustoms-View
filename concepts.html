Logical Brief

Read here for all your doubts

Our first project is based on the morse code, which was used in wars earlier, with an idea of converting every number or alphabet into dots and dashes, so that the message can be conveyed in the form of long and short taps. Every morse code motorcycle will be based on a unique number that will be assigned to client and the same message will be conveyed over the bike in the form of stripes or the pattern of light which can be recognised, giving the motorcycle a unique identity.There will be only 27 motorcycles that will be coverted into morse code as a tribute to the inventor of the code, Sameul Morse, an american painter and inventor (born on 27th April 1791).
Keeping the radicality in mind, the concept has been evolved reflecting minimalism and the expression of abstraction as well, because that is what the morse code conveys with the idea of simplifying every sentence and word into dots and dashes. The bike can be a turned into a scrambler, cafe racer or a naked street as per the request of the customer.
